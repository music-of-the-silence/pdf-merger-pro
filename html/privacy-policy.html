Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 4, 2024

Welcome to PDF Merger Pro. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your data when you use our website and tell you about your privacy rights.

1. Information We Don't Collect

PDF Merger Pro is designed with your privacy in mind. We want to be very clear about what we don't collect:

  • We do not collect or store any of the PDF files you process through our tool.
  • We do not track your browsing activity across other websites.
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or IP addresses.

2. How Your Data is Processed

All file processing happens directly in your web browser:

  • Your PDF files are processed locally on your device.
  • No files are uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • Your files remain completely private and are never stored or transmitted over the internet.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us improve our service. Google Analytics may collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, but this information is anonymized.
  • Cloudflare: We use Cloudflare for content delivery and DDoS protection. They may collect and process certain information about your visit to our website.

4. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@pdfmergerpro.example.com.
